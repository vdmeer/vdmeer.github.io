Research Experience

1995 – 1996 Research Student, Institute for Open Communication Systems, GMD FOKUS, Berlin, Germany
  • developed an ISDN call manager implementing an network layer state machine based on an existing link layer API
  • integrated the call manager into a personal communication system based on OSI stack
  • developed a location-based ISDN telephony service
1997 – 2002 Research Assistant and Ph.D. Student, Department for Open Communication Systems (OKS), Technical University Berlin, Germany
  • developed 3 M.Sc. courses (management of distributed systems, broadband networks and mobile communications)
  • coordinated 2 main project courses (personal communication support and unified messaging)
  • co-managed all interactions with students for lecturers and projects
1997 – 2002 Co-head of Competence Center for Open Communication Systems (OKS), FhG FOKUS, Berlin, Germany
  • co-developed the concept of I-Centric Communications, the main research focus of FOKUS for 4 years.
  • managed projects for developing intelligent communication sytems, unified messaging, media gateways and service personalisation– co-managed the design and development of a Unified Messaging System
  • designed the initial architecture for IST AlbatrOSS
  • developed an E-Learning course for the TEN-A project
2002 – 2003 Postdoctoral Researcher, Telecommunications Software & System Group (TSSG), Waterford Institute of Technology (WIT), Ireland
  • managed HEA basic research programme M-Zones
  • coordinated student work on research projects and strengthened M.Sc. programme
  • cooperated in defining the curriculum of an elective for B.Sc. programme
  • actively cooperated in EU Framework project proposals and running projects
2003 – 2004 Investigator, Telecommunications Software & System Group (TSSG), Waterford Institute of Technology (WIT), Ireland
  • managed HEA basic research programme M-Zones
  • participated in SFI grant to develop a PI cluster on autonomic management
  • cooperated in development of Ph.D. programme
  • actively cooperated in EU Framework project proposals and running projects
2004 – 2009 Senior Investigator, Telecommunications Software & System Group (TSSG), Waterford Institute of Technology (WIT), Ireland
  • scientific lead on architecture and modelling work in SFI PI Cluster on autonomic management (AMCNS)
  • coordinated cooperation with industry (namely Motorola Labs, Chicago)
  • participated in standardisation (namely in TM Forum architecture and modelling groups and in the ACF)
  • designed scientific programme for HEA project FutureCom
  • supervised Ph.D. students
  • participated in a number of EU framework projects (namely Auto-I)
  • advised scientific work on Celtic project on autonomic management (MADEIRA, in cooperation with Ericsson and Siemens)
  • consulted for a number of TSSG commercialisation projects (namely ASYST, I-Serv, and Zimbie)
2009 – 2011 Senior Research Fellow, Telecommunications Software & System Group (TSSG), Waterford Institute of Technology (WIT), Ireland
  • Funded Investigator, Programme Manager and Leader of Work Package 2 (Architectures, Models and Algorithms) in SFI Strategic Research Cluster FAME
  • developing architectural artefacts and processes for business-to-network translation and semantic interoperability (in cooperation with POSTECH, HP and Cisco)
  • designed federation architecture (in cooperation with HP)
  • established distributed testbed for federated policy-based management (in cooperation with Cisco)
2011 - to date Master Engineer, Team Leader, Ericsson NM-Lab, Ericsson, Ireland
