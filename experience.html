Experience

Across all organisations, work comprised research (projects), teaching and supervision, publications, talks and presentations, and conference organisation and reviews. The research projects are grouped by organisation. I used the same grouping for teaching and supervision. For publications, I tried to use the standard BiBLaTeX categories.
