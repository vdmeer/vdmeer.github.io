RFCs: None-Technical

Some RFCs are not as serious as others. Beside the early stages, where poems made it into the internet standards, almost every year there is one (or more) RFCs for April fool’s day. Look out for the publication on "April 1" in the RFC index, all normal RFCs do not carry the day of publication but only the month. And some RFCs are provide guidance and principles beyond the technical standards. Here is a list of them.
